About Me

Student Fullstack .NET at Chas Academy - Stockholm, Sweden.

Biography

I am a dedicated and responsible person with experience in sales, healthcare, and leadership. My passion for combining medical technology with dynamic and hands-on environments led me to pursue a career as a radiology nurse/Radiographer.

Over the past seven years, I have worked in emergency care at Karolinska University Hospital, where I held various roles, both clinical and managerial.

However, balancing a demanding healthcare career with personal life has not always been easy, and I have long been drawn to the field of IT. This sparked my decision to take a bold step forward and pursue studies in full-stack software development.

With my diverse professional background, a strong desire to learn, and a genuine passion for technology, I am excited to build innovative solutions that bridge my past experiences with my future aspirations.
